Welcome to Wreck Racing

Our Purpose

Wreck Racing is an all-student racing team that builds cars for the Grassroots Motorsports $2000 Challenge. With a tight budget, the $2000 Challenge favors those with more ingenuity and skill than money. As many as 100 different teams return each year to battle it out and see who can squeeze the most performance from a penny.

Our Members

There are typically three types of people who join Wreck Racing:

I. Aspiring Enthusiasts

These members may know little about cars but are eager to learn. Whether it's driving, fixing, or general maintenance, they join to gain hands-on experience with real cars and apply classroom theory to real-world situations.

II. Experienced Gearheads

These members are knowledgeable about everything from maintenance to high-performance mods. They often drive fast cars and join for the unique challenge of building a race-ready car on a tight budget.

III. Total Car Freaks

These car enthusiasts likely own multiple budget cars and have done engine swaps on both. Wreck Racing provides them with an outlet for their passion while at school.

Regardless of skill level, Wreck Racing welcomes all who love cars. Members can get hands-on experience with designing, machining, welding, wiring, testing, and more. Come visit our garage—you don’t know what you’re missing!
